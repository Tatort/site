The Allcology lab at the University of Florida's [Nature Coast Biological Station](https://ncbs.ifas.ufl.edu/) is dedicated to the study of all-things-ecology. We utilize state-of-the-art technology and modern methods to answer a variety of questions related to species conservation. Our projects range from applied to theoretical and mostly deal with imperiled species and their population status and threats.




It makes use of a variety of open source projects including:

* [Cobra](https://github.com/spf13/cobra)
* [Viper](https://github.com/spf13/viper)
* [J Walter Weatherman](https://github.com/spf13/jWalterWeatherman)
* [Cast](https://github.com/spf13/cast)

Learn more and contribute on [GitHub](https://github.com/spf13).
