The Allcology lab at the University of Florida's [Nature Coast Biological Station](https://ncbs.ifas.ufl.edu/) is dedicated to the study of all-things-ecology. We utilize state-of-the-art technology and modern methods to answer a variety of questions related to species conservation. Our projects range from applied to theoretical and mostly deal with imperiled species and their population status.
